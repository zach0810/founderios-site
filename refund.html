Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated:

This policy applies to consulting services provided by Confident Golfer Inc, operating as Founder iOS. We want every client relationship to be clear and fair. The terms below describe how billing, cancellation, and refunds work.

1. Free Consultation First

Every engagement begins with a free consultation so both sides can confirm fit before any money changes hands. You are never charged for the initial consult, and no payment is collected until you agree to begin a paid engagement.

2. Monthly Engagements

Month-to-month consulting is billed in advance at the start of each service period. You may cancel at any time by emailing zach@founderios.com before your next billing date. Cancellation stops future billing; the current paid month continues through the end of its service period. We do not prorate or refund a month already in progress, because the consulting time has been reserved for you.

3. Prepaid Engagements

If you choose a prepaid term (for example, a 3-month or 6-month engagement at a reduced monthly rate), the term is billed in full at signing. Prepaid engagements may be cancelled in writing, and we will refund the unused portion of the prepaid term calculated at the standard monthly rate of $2,000/month for the months already delivered, less any sessions already completed. Because prepaid rates are a discount for commitment, refunds reflect the standard (non-discounted) rate for time already used.

4. How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

Send your request to zach@founderios.com. We will confirm receipt and process any eligible refund to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

5. Disputes

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us first so we can resolve it directly and quickly. We are committed to fair treatment and will work with you in good faith.
