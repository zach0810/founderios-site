Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Confident Golfer Inc ("Founder iOS," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or engage our consulting services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, phone number, and business name when you book a call or contact us.
  • Business information — details about your company that you share during consultations and engagements.
  • Payment information — when you become a paying client, payment details are collected and processed by our third-party payment processor. We do not store full card numbers on our own systems.

We may also collect limited technical data automatically, such as basic analytics about how visitors use this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and conduct consultations and deliver our consulting services.
  • To process payments and manage your engagement.
  • To communicate with you about your engagement, scheduling, and billing.
  • To improve our services and respond to inquiries.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely by third-party payment processors. When you make a payment, your card information is submitted directly to the processor and is subject to its own privacy and security practices. We receive confirmation of payment but do not store your complete card details.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate our business (such as payment processors, scheduling, and email tools), and only as needed to deliver our services. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard the data you entrust to us.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes are reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
